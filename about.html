Our Story

In the corridors of high school, where dreams take root and passions ignite, Bloom 9js had its humble genesis. It was a time when the weight of textbooks clashed with the allure of melodies, and young hearts dared to dream beyond the classroom. In the midst of algebra and history lessons, a group of friends found solace in the world of music. Late-night jam sessions in dimly lit garages, the strumming of old guitars, and the rhythmic cadence of handwritten lyrics became their sanctuary. It was here, amidst the clutter of teenage dreams, that Bloom 9js began to bloom. Life as students came with its own set of challenges. Balancing calculus equations and composing melodies posed a unique kind of juggling act. There were nights when the whisper of inspiration arrived uninvited, demanding attention in the midst of exam preparation. Yet, those were the moments that shaped the foundation of Bloom 9js. It was a journey fueled by relentless determination and an unwavering belief in the power of music. The early days were marked by makeshift recording studios and makeshift budgets. Every note was a triumph, every gig a milestone. The warmth of encouragement from friends and family acted as a beacon, guiding Bloom 9js through the labyrinth of uncertainties.
